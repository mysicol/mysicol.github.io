THOUGHTS AND OPINIONS

philosophy

definition of the self.

My current philosophy is that the entire universe is an extension of the self. This is not a self-centered perspective. It answers a question proposed in "I think therefore I am". This point provides no evidence that the rest of the universe exists beyond your own imagination. Therefore we can define the entire universe as being completely contained by my own understanding. This definition provides sufficient evidence for the existence of the universe.

This results in significant questions and pain on loss.
